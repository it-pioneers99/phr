## Saudi Labor Law Quick Reference

| Years of Service | Resignation | Termination by Employer |
|------------------|-------------|------------------------|
| < 2 years | Nothing | Half month/year (first 5) + Full month/year (after 5) |
| 2-5 years | 1/3 of full | Half month/year (first 5) + Full month/year (after 5) |
| 5-10 years | 2/3 of full | Half month/year (first 5) + Full month/year (after 5) |
| >= 10 years | Full entitlement | Half month/year (first 5) + Full month/year (after 5) |
